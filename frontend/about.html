Our Story

From Kigali to the World

KigaliCraft was founded in 2020 with a simple but powerful mission: to connect Rwanda's extraordinary artisans with customers around the world who appreciate authentic, handcrafted products.

Rwanda has a rich tradition of craftsmanship passed down through generations. From the iconic agaseke peace baskets to the geometric imigongo paintings, every piece tells a story of culture, skill, and pride.

We work directly with over 120 artisans across 30 districts, ensuring fair prices and sustainable livelihoods for the talented creators behind each product.
